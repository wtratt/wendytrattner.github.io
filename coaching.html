About Me


Hey there, I'm Wendy – an artist and personal wellness coach with a past life as a hardware engineer at various cleantech startups. I came into wellness coaching after a long journey of career pivots, financial struggles, and several existential and identity crises. I've learned the hard way how to establish healthy habits and relationships, find the right career path, accumulate wealth, build confidence, and overcome my own challenges to live a happier, more authentic life. Now I want to help fast-track others to design their lives and become the most joyful version of themselves.

I am always learning and have completed various workshops on authentic relating and helping others to problem solve. However, I also believe experience is a great teacher.

In my own life, I’ve navigated:
-3 career pivots
-6 serious relationships & a LOT of dating
-being a high achieving MIT engineer
-being the only woman in the room
-loss of loved ones
-being financially independent since age 15
-learning about personal finance and becoming an almost-millionaire by age 25
-building my confidence from zero
-starting an art business
-realizing the thing that gave me a feeling of ‘purpose’ was also the thing causing my unhappiness
-building healthy routines and habits
-going from depression to profound wellness
-quitting engineering in pursuit of joy
-living the life I actually wanted and wasn’t allowing myself to want
-helping my friends with relationships, careers, confidence, and finances
